Why Most People Fail to Get I.T.

Posted on Jan 18, 2022

The Information Technology field is immensely vast, however, one can break it down in three core aspects.

What is IT?

  • Programming
  • Computer Networking
  • Operating Systems

The problem

The issue is that most people, even ones on the field, fail to grasp the basics. They try to invent theories on what technology is, and end up abstracting what Information Technology really is all about. Information Technology is not Object-Oriented Programming, it is not knowing the history of a programming language, it is not touch-screen tablets in schools. IT is not social studies and humanities, it is all about engineering, science and math, it involves logic reasoning without a place for ambiguities.
